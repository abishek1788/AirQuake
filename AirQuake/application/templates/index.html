Welcome to Air Quake Simulations! Basically this is a company that I created selling F18 Simulators. Started in January of 2020 when I finally decided to build an F-18C Cockpit from scratch. Development took me about a year, and spent a while getting the quality to something I would be proud to sell. This is the home page, with some pictures of my products. The portfolio page has each individual product I am selling. The Dashboards, Expense Demo, and Chart pages are experimental pages for testing out the database this website is connected to. It is supposed to replicate an expense/profit tracker with my own tweaks. You can enter your expenses/profits in the Expense Demo page, then you can see/delete them from the dashboards page. To see charts generated, refer to the charts page and it will give you a few different types of graphs for tracking the data that was entered.
